DATUMMARK — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 8 March 2026

DATA COLLECTION

DatumMark does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.

NETWORK REQUESTS

DatumMark makes zero network requests. The app is entirely offline. All IALA buoyage data is bundled at compile time. No servers are contacted, ever.

LOCAL STORAGE

The app stores your theme and IALA region preferences locally on your device using standard iOS UserDefaults. No other data is persisted. Quiz scores are not saved between sessions.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

DatumMark does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. There are zero third-party SDKs. Zero external dependencies.

CHILDREN

DatumMark is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

CHANGES

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

CONTACT

For questions about this privacy policy or the app, please open an issue on GitHub.
