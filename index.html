DATUMMARK — SUPPORT

OVERVIEW

DatumMark is an IALA Maritime Buoyage System reference and exam revision app. It covers all lateral, cardinal, isolated danger, safe water, special, and emergency wreck marks for both IALA Region A and Region B. All data is bundled — no internet connection required.

MARKS TAB

Browse all sea marks organised by category. Each mark shows an accurate colour swatch, full colour description, topmark details, and light characteristics. Tap any mark to view a rendered buoy with hull colours, topmark, and light rhythm animation.

REFERENCE TAB

Quick Reference Card showing all marks at a glance. Light rhythm reference with descriptions and example marks. Buoy shape and topmark shape glossaries.

SEARCH TAB

Full-text search across mark names, colour descriptions, light characteristics, and group names. Results update as you type.

QUIZ TAB

RYA-style exam practice. Identify marks from rendered buoy images in sessions of 10 questions. Pass mark is 7/10. Three modes: identify by name, identify by light character, and identify by colour description. Questions respect your IALA Region selection.

CONFIG TAB

Choose between amber, green, or red display themes. Red preserves scotopic (night-adapted) vision — recommended for bridge use during hours of darkness. Select IALA Region A or B. Your preferences are saved between sessions.

TROUBLESHOOTING

  • Marks look wrong for my region: Check the IALA Region setting in the CONFIG tab. Lateral marks swap colours between Region A and Region B.
  • Quiz seems too hard: Review the Quick Reference Card in the REF tab first. Focus on the distinguishing features — colour bands, topmarks, and light characters.
